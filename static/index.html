How to Play SixJax

Welcome to SixJax Poker

SixJax is a 6-card, point-based poker game for 2–6 players with no wagering. Build the best 5-card poker hand from your 6 cards to win points from your opponents.

♠
The Goal
Win hands to earn points. The player with the best 5-card poker hand wins the round.
🃏
6 Cards, Not 5
Each player has 6 cards. Your best 5-card combination is automatically selected.
🔄
Draw & Decide
On your turn, draw a card and choose: replace one of your cards, or burn it and reveal a face-down card.

How a Hand Works

1
Deal
Each player gets 6 cards — 2 face-up and 4 face-down. No players see the face-down cards until they are revealed.
2
Draw Phase
On your turn, draw a card from the deck. You'll see it before deciding what to do.
3
Choose Your Action
Replace: Swap the drawn card with any card in your hand (replaced card goes to discard).
Burn & Reveal: Discard the drawn card and optionally flip a face-down card face-up.
4
Final Turns
When the deck runs out, each player gets one final turn. Then all cards are revealed.
5
Scoring
The player with the best hand wins points from each opponent. See the Scoring tab for details.

How Scoring Works

The winner earns points from each losing player, not a flat amount.

Base Points

The winner receives 3 points from each loser.
Example: In a 4-player game, the winner gets 3 × 3 = 9 points.

Bonus Points

The winner also earns bonus points per opponent based on hand quality difference.

For each opponent:
bonus = max(0, winner_bonus − opponent_bonus)

Point Multiplier

The room's point value multiplies all points. A 2x room doubles everything.
